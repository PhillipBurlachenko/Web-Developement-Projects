Starting Out!

Purchase a Fishing License!

To get into fishing, you must first purchase a license because it is illegal to fish without one! Almost all public waters are governed by the Fish and Wildlife organization. The purchase of a license helps fund this organization that protects our wildlife. Luckily the licenses are affordable, and the Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife made it easy to purchase one!

Purchasing a Fishing License Online!

The Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife makes it easy to purchase a license online through their website. If you are interested, please click the link below to be directed to their page.

Visit Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife License System

Purchasing a Fishing License through a Vendor!

The Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife enables people to also purchase a fishing license from vendors. Examples of some vendors that can sell licenses are:

  • Walmart
  • Frey Meyers
  • Big 5 Sporting Goods
  • Dicks Sporting Outdoors
  • Cabelas
  • Bass Pro Shops
  • Sportsman Warehouse
  • Target

The Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife website also has a license vendor system that allows you to search for WDFW authorized licensed sales locations near you. Please click the link below if you are interested in being directed.

Visit Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife License Vendor System
