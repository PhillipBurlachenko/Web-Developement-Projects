What is Fishing The Pacific Northwest?

FishingPNW.com is your online resource for all things fishing in the great state of Washington. Here you will find all that you need to know to get out and go fishing!

Why Fishing?

Fishing is one of the most accessible outdoor sports, and has been around since the early days of mankind. It does not matter your age, income level, fitness ability or gender, anybody can easily participate.


Fishing can make you a better person! It can keep you physically fit, help you live longer, reduce stress, decrease symptoms of PTSD, and overall helps you unplug.
